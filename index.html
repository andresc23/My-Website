I am a first-generation Mexican-American, originally from Las Lomas CA. I grew up in both Las Lomas and in Watsonville CA.

I attended Santa Clara University and got my Bachelor's of Science degree in Computer Science and Engineering on June 2020.

I currently live in Santa Clara, and I am continually looking for work, while also working on ways to improve my skills and knowledge as a software developer.
